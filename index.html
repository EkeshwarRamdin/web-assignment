Welcome to Rodrigues Island . Rodrigues Island is one of the best Islands which have beautiful beaches and fabulous network of walking trails.

Discover variety of fun and exciting tours , excursion and packages from sight seeing tours to day cruises, ziplines excursions and many more.

Rodrigues is one of the best multi fishing destinations in the world , surrouded by a large continental plateau.

Known for its rich biodiversity, Rodrigues Island was once flourished with an abundant of endemic plant and animal species. It was home to now extinct flightless bird, the Rodrigues solitaire.
